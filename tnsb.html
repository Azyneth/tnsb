TUCHONG NANING SDN. BHD.

Tuchong Naning Sdn. Bhd. is a company incorporated on 10 June 2015 under the the Companies Acts, 1965 and is wholly owned by a Sarawakian, Bumiputera entrepreneur.

The director/share holder of the company, are certified civil engineer. His experiences acquired have given him the capacities and certainty to frame and manage the company. This speaks to a certifiable exertion by the company to react to the government's calling expanding Bumiputera participation in building and construction industries.

The company plans to diversify its future business operation into the following fields;
~ Property Development     ~ Land Development (eg. Oil Palm Plantation)  

~ Reforestation Development     ~ Mechanical Works, Electrical Power Services & ICT Services.
